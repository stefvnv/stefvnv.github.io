Computerium
Overview

The Computerium desktop application demonstrates advanced object-oriented programming concepts, including inheritance, polymorphism, and aggregation. This application allows users to assemble a custom PC by selecting various PC parts, choosing shipping methods, and viewing the total price in their cart. It showcases the power of Python's object-oriented capabilities.


Features

Extensive Part Selection

Computerium offers users a wide variety of PC parts to choose from, including CPUs, coolers, graphics cards, motherboards, memory, storage devices, and power supplies. Each part category includes multiple options for customization.


Dynamic Part Details

As users navigate through different part categories, the application dynamically updates part details, providing relevant information specific to the selected part type. This dynamic behavior is achieved through the use of polymorphism, enhancing user experience and clarity.


Efficient Shopping Cart

Users can easily add selected parts to their shopping cart. The application calculates the total price, including both the part prices and shipping costs, allowing users to keep track of their expenses.


Cart Management

Computerium supports cart management features, enabling users to view the contents of their cart, empty it, and receive notifications confirming successful actions. This simplifies the shopping process and ensures a smooth user experience.

Target Audience

Computerium caters to users interested in building their custom PCs by selecting specific components tailored to their needs. It serves as a valuable educational tool for those looking to understand object-oriented programming concepts such as inheritance, polymorphism, and aggregation in Python.


Compatibility

The Computerium desktop application is compatible with Windows operating systems, offering a user-friendly and educational experience to Python enthusiasts and programming learners alike.
