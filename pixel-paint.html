Pixel Paint
Story

Join Pixel on an unexpected journey as he awakens in a simulated world. With your help, he must decipher the puzzle and find his way back to Pixelia, facing challenges, enemies, and mind-bending puzzles along the way.


Concept and Genre

Test your problem-solving skills in this engaging puzzle/platformer. In Pixel Paint, players step into the colorful world of Pixel, a character who must navigate a pixelated dimension by avoiding stepping on the wrong color blocks. It's a unique blend of puzzle and platformer genres where players must think on their feet while jumping, running, and outwitting enemies.


Gameplay

In Pixel Paint, you'll master various skills, including running, jumping, changing colors, wall jumps, crate manipulation, and key unlocking, all while avoiding missteps on the wrong color. The game features ten progressively challenging levels, each introducing a new skill. The thrilling final arcade-style level lets you perform air jumps to aid Pixel in his escape.

Touch gems to switch color

Run away from chasing enemies


Setting

Pixel Paint unfolds in a vibrant, pixelated dimension filled with optimism and color. Explore this charming world as you guide Pixel on his quest to break free from the simulation and return to Pixelia, his home planet.


Target Audience

Pixel Paint offers a light-hearted, non-violent experience suitable for all age groups. It's an ideal choice for indie game fans, puzzle/platformer enthusiasts, and players aged 10 to 35.


Compatibility

Pixel Paint is compatible with Windows PCs, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of players.
