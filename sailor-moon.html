Sailor Moon
Story

Take on the role of Sailor Moon, the guardian of love and justice, as she embarks on a mission to save the galaxy. Battle through hordes of enemies, culminating in an intense showdown with the final boss. Follow the narrative through two fading cutscenes, adding depth to the story.


Concept and Genre

Test your platforming skills in this exciting Sailor Moon adventure. Join Sailor Moon on a quest to save the galaxy as you navigate platforms, defeat enemies, and face the ultimate showdown. This action-packed platformer with shooting elements is a must-play for Sailor Moon fans and platform game enthusiasts.


Gameplay

In the Sailor Moon Platform Game, you control Sailor Moon, using precise movements and quick reflexes to dodge, jump, shoot, and defeat enemies. Explore six challenging levels, each bringing you closer to an epic boss battle. Collect power-ups, ammo, and hearts for extra lives as you progress.

Setting

Immerse yourself in the Sailor Moon universe, with vibrant and detailed pixel art. Experience the magical world of Sailor Moon as you journey through six captivating levels.


Target Audience

Sailor Moon offers an exciting and family-friendly gaming experience suitable for all ages. It's a nostalgic adventure for fans of the series and a thrilling platformer for gamers of all ages.


Compatibility

Sailor Moon Platform Game is designed for Windows PCs, offering an authentic and enjoyable gaming experience.
