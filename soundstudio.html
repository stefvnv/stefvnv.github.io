Audacity Project 1

For this project, I used the instrumental of a song I really love, ‘Pretty When You Cry’ by Lana Del Rey from her album 'Ultraviolence' to recreate the bridge of the song. Due to the guitar, harmony, and syncopated rhythms, the original song has quite a jazzy feel to it. In order to recreate this I recorded myself, then overdubbed another recording of my voice. For effects, I used the echo, low-pass filter, reverb & wahwah filters and added a fade out at the end.

'Pretty When You Cry' by Lana Del Rey

Audacity Project 2

For this project, I used the intro of ‘Montreal’ by The Weeknd from his album 'Trilogy'. The song already has a certain intensity to it that almost creates fear, however I wanted to enhance that even more. I added a fade in at the beginning and fade out at the end. I added delay on certain parts of the sample, and increased the bass & reduced the treble. Finally, I added a copy of the song on another layer, and increased the pitch on certain lines to make it sound like there is a second voice harmonizing.

'Montreal' by The Weeknd
