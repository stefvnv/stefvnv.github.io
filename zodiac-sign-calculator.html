Zodiac Sign Calculator
Overview

The Python Zodiac Sign Calculator is a user-friendly application that provides an easy way for users to determine their astrological and Chinese zodiac signs. With a straightforward interface, user management capabilities, and convenient options, this application simplifies the process of finding and managing zodiac sign information.


Features

Zodiac Sign Calculation

Discover your astrological zodiac sign based on your birthdate, including day and month. Optionally, calculate your Chinese zodiac sign by entering your birth year.


User Management

Create and delete user profiles to keep track of zodiac sign information for yourself and others, making it ideal for families or individuals interested in astrology.


Options Menu

Access a user-friendly menu to reset input fields, uncheck the Chinese Zodiac option, and clear previous zodiac sign calculations. Easily exit the application when done.


User-Friendly Interface

Navigate the application with ease through an intuitive graphical user interface (GUI). Input birthdate details, calculate zodiac signs, and manage user profiles effortlessly.

Target Audience

This application caters to individuals of all ages interested in astrology. Whether you're exploring your astrological traits or looking to calculate zodiac signs for family and friends, the Python Zodiac Sign Calculator offers a user-friendly solution.


Compatibility

The Zodiac Sign Calculator is designed to run on Windows operating systems, providing a familiar and accessible environment for users across various Windows versions, including Windows 7, 8, 10, and later.
